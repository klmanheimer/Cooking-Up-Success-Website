About the makings of the game

The goal of our game was to create an engaging experience where players can manage and grow their own business. From the start, our objective was to simulate real-world decision-making in a fun, interactive way. We began by outlining key gameplay mechanics—such as budgeting, purchasing, and scaling operations—to reflect the challenges of starting and running a business.To bring our goal to life, we focused on simple, intuitive controls and a progression system that rewards smart decisions. Players start with a limited budget and must make strategic choices on how to spend it—whether on ingredients, equipment, or marketing. These choices directly affect profits and growth, reinforcing the importance of planning and adaptability.Throughout development, we stayed true to our core idea: teaching basic business concepts through play. The result is a game that’s not only entertaining but also offers players insight into entrepreneurship and problem-solving.
