About the Game

In this game… you’re the boss. You are responsible for the start-up and opening of a local pizzeria. You are given a certain amount of money to purchase items such as, kitchen equipment, ingredients, etc. for your pizzeria. The goal is for you to scale and grow a company while also getting the experience of making decisions that have real-time effects on the business and the money you make. Do you have what it takes to run and scale a business? Find out and give it a shot for yourself!

Team Goal

Cooking Up Success Many students struggle to balance their food budgets, often admitting to impulse buying and lacking confidence in their budgeting skills. Group 5, the Landlocked Ligers, set out to address this issue by designing a game that encourages healthier financial and impulse decisions in a fun and engaging way. Our goal was to create an interactive experience that helps students prioritize nutritious eating without overspending. We wanted to highlight the real-life challenges of managing limited resources while promoting strategic thinking and planning. Through gameplay, we hoped to provide both entertainment and education—offering a space where players can practice and strengthen their budgeting skills in a low-risk environment. progression of the game.
