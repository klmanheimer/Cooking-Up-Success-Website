Cooking Up Success

Give it a Try

interactive game demo, or
video showing game play

In this game… you’re the boss. You are responsible for the start-up and opening of a local pizzeria. You are given a certain amount of money to purchase items such as, kitchen equipment, ingredients, etc. for your pizzeria. The goal is for you to scale and grow a company while also getting the experience of making decisions that have real-time effects on the business and the money you make. Do you have what it takes to run and scale a business? Find out and give it a shot for yourself!
